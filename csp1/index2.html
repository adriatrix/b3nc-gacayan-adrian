About

[ what is AoV? ]

Arena of Valor

Arena of Valor or AoV is an epic new multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game by Garena.

Classic 5v5 MOBA, Perfected for Mobile

Battle in a familiar three-lane map filled with brushes, nooks and crannies that allow you to pull off petmalu kills.

Fast Matchmaking & 15-Minute Matches

Enjoy fast matchmaking with players around the world and become a legend!

The Ultimate MOBA Experience

With intuitive controls tailored for mobile, you will dominate the battlefield in no time!

Play as any hero or legend

Explore and command a roster of over 30 heroes and counting.
